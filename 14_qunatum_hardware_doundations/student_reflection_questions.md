# Student Reflection Questions

1. In what way is a transistor similar to a qubit device?
2. In what way is a transistor fundamentally different from a qubit device?
3. Why does a superconducting qubit require a Josephson junction?
4. Why is nonlinearity important for selecting two qubit states?
5. What does a microwave pulse do to a transmon qubit?
6. Why does measurement require classical electronics?
7. Why is a quantum computer still a hybrid classical-quantum system?
8. Why is the hardware challenge as important as the algorithm challenge?
9. What physical constraints limit the number of usable quantum gates?
10. What is the difference between a Qiskit gate and the physical pulse that implements it?
