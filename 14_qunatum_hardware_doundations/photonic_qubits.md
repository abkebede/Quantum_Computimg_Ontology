# Photonic Qubits

Photonic qubits use light. Information may be encoded in polarization, path, time-bin, or frequency modes.

## Physical basis

A photon can carry a qubit through two distinguishable modes.

## Control

Beam splitters, phase shifters, interferometers, nonlinear media, and measurement-induced operations manipulate photonic states.

## Measurement

Single-photon detectors record photon arrivals.

## Strengths

- Natural communication carriers
- Room-temperature propagation possible
- Useful for quantum networking and sensing

## Challenges

- Deterministic two-qubit gates are difficult without strong nonlinearity or measurement-based schemes
- Photon loss is a major issue
- High-efficiency sources and detectors are essential
